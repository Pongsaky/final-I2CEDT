What is TDEE?

: TDEE is stand for "Total Daily Energy Expenditure"

In short, It's an approximation of Energy you use in a day.

Some simple equation of TDEE

TDEE = [ 10 x weight (kg) + 6.25 x height (cm) - 5 x age (years) + 5 ] x Activity factor

What is BMR?

: BMR or "Basal Metabolic Rate"

BMR represents the minimum amount of energy (in calories) your body requires to keep you alive and functioning while you're in a state of complete rest, such as when you're asleep

How to calculate the BMR?

Female : 655 + (9.6 x weight in kg) + (1.8 x height in cm) - (4.7 x age in years)

Male : 66 + (13.7 x weight in kg) + (5 x height in cm) - (6.8 x age in years)

What are the factors

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Body Composition (muscle mass versus fat)
  • Genetics
