How Body Burns Calories?

: By "Metabolism process", Metabolism is process of converting food to energy for entire body system e.g. Circulatory system, Respiratory system

How exercise make body burns more calories?

Any exercise requires a constant supply of energy which mean body will use more energy.


What is TDEE?

: TDEE is stand for "Total Daily Energy Expenditure"

In short, It's an approximation of Energy you use in a day.

An Application of TDEE for Losing Weight

"If we consume slightly less energy than TDEE every day, you will slowly slimmer"

What are The Factors?

  • Sex
  • Age
  • Weight
  • Height
  • Activity
  • Body Fat

Some simple equation of TDEE

TDEE = [ 10 x weight (kg) + 6.25 x height (cm) - 5 x age (years) + 5 ] x Activity factor


What is BMR?

: BMR or "Basal Metabolic Rate"

BMR represents the minimum amount of energy (in calories) your body requires to keep you alive and functioning while you're in a state of complete rest, such as when you're asleep

How to calculate the BMR?

Female : 655 + (9.6 x weight in kg) + (1.8 x height in cm) - (4.7 x age in years)

Male : 66 + (13.7 x weight in kg) + (5 x height in cm) - (6.8 x age in years)

What are the factors

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Body Composition (muscle mass versus fat)
  • Genetics

What is "Activity Level"?

: A rough indicator to show how active you are

What are each level represented?

    Sedentary: Only do a daily activity e.g., walking, taking out the trash.

    Lightly Active: Doing some intentionally light exercise e.g., slow jogging

    Moderately Active: Doing an intentional exercise everyday e.g., Jogging for at least 50 minutes

    Very Active: You spend most of the day doing something physical e.g., physical job, athlete or walking for at least 4 hours.
